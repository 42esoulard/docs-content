If you are the [owner](/console/my-account/concepts#owner) of an [Organization](/console/my-account/concepts#organization), the **Deactivate Organization** option will display at the bottom of the Organization Account page.

Deactivating an Organization will permanently delete your account and all of its services and backups.

<Message type="requirement">

  - You have an account and are logged into the [Scaleway Console](https://console.scaleway.com) 

</Message>

<Message type="note">

There is a delay of up to 10 days between the time your organization is closed and the time resources are effectively destroyed. It is recommended to cancel all your resources before you close your Organization. A deactivated organization can be reactivated upon your request.To unlock the different levels, you will need to progressively add information to your account.

</Message> 


<Lightbox src="scaleway-deactivate-organization.png" />

1. Click **Deactivate Organization** once you have read and agree with the warning message.

2. Type **Deactivate** in the first box to confirm your decision. You can also fill out the second box with the reason you decided to close your account.

3. Click **Deactivate Organization permanently** to confirm.
