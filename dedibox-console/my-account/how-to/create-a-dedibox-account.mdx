[Scaleway Dedibox](https://www.scaleway.com/en/dedibox/) are our high quality dedicated servers. With over 15 years experience, we provide dedicated servers which are recognized for their extreme reliability and best-in-class redundancy. We manage a continuously growing park of more than 100,000 servers in our datacenters in France and the Netherlands. This quality allows us to offer up to 99.95% SLA.

## How to create a Scaleway Dedibox account

1. Open the [Scaleway Dedibox](https://www.scaleway.com/en/dedibox/) website in a web browser.

2. Click **Sign up** in the top right corner of the website:

    <Lightbox src="scaleway-dedibox-signup.png" width="750" alt="" />

3. Fill in the required information, validate the terms of service, and click **Create my account**. A link is sent to your e-mail.

    <Lightbox src="scaleway-dedibox-subscribe.png" width="750" alt="" />

4. Click the link to validate your email address and to activate your account. The [Dedibox Console](https://console.online.net) login screen displays.

5. Enter your chosen username and password and click **Sign In**:

    <Lightbox src="scaleway-dedibox-login.png" width="750" alt="" />

You are now connected to the Scaleway Dedibox Console and you can order your first dedicated server:

    <Lightbox src="scaleway-dedibox-console.png" width="750" alt="" />

## How to add a payment method

You will be asked to add a payment method during the order process of your first Dedibox. If you want to add a payment method before you order a product, you can do it as follows:

1. When logged into the Scaleway [Dedibox Console](https://console.online.net) click your user name next to _logged in as:_ in the top right corner of the console. Then click **Billing** in the pop-up menu:

    <Lightbox src="scaleway-dedibox-popup.png" width="250" alt="" />

2. The billing page displays. Click **Change payment mode**:

    <Lightbox src="scaleway-dedibox-paymentmode.png" width="750" alt="" />

3. Choose your payment modee from the list and click **Update payment mode**:

    <Lightbox src="scaleway-dedibox-new-paymentmode.png" width="750" alt="" />

    <Message type="note">

    Not all payment methods may be available for your account.

    </Message>

4. Enter the details of your new payment mode and validate.
