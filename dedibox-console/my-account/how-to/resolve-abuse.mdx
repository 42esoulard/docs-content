An abuse ticket is a complaint for hosting copyrighted content or illegal behaviour using Scaleway services.

<Message type="requirement">

  - You have an account and are logged into the [Dedibox Console](https://console.online.net) 

</Message>

<Message type="important">

If you receive an abuse ticket, you **must** solve the issue within 48 hours to avoid your services being locked.

</Message>

1. Click your user name next to _logged in as:_ in the top right corner of the console. Then click **[Abuses](https://console-sudo.infra.online.net/en/account/abuses/list)** in the pop-up menu.

  <Lightbox src="scaleway-dedibox-console-abuses-menu.png" width="300" alt="" />

2. A list of your abuse tickets displays. Click **Details** to see the details of the complaint:

  <Lightbox src="scaleway-dedibox-console-abuses-list.png" width="800" alt="" />

3. Take note of the details and complete the steps necessary to resolve the problem. You can find information on  how to resolve [outgoing DDoS](https://www.scaleway.com/en/docs/how-to-prevent-outgoing-ddos/) attacks from your server or stop [sending malicious emails](https://www.scaleway.com/en/docs/stopping-outgoing-spam/) in our documentation.

4. Once the issue is solved, describe the measures you have taken and provide an answer to the author of the ticket. Click **Resolve** to mark the ticket as closed.

<Message type="note">

More information about Scaleway's abuse policy can be found [here](https://www.scaleway.com/en/abuse-notice/).

</Message>
