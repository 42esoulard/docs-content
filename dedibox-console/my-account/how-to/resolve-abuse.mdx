# How to resolve Abuse tickets

An abuse ticket is a complaint for hosting copyrighted content or illegal behaviour using Scaleway services.

> **Important:** If you receive an abuse ticket, you must solve the issue within 48 hours to avoid your services being locked.

To resolve the abuse ticket:

1. Log into your Scaleway Dedibox [Console](https://console.online.net).

2. Once logged in, click your user name next to _logged in as:_ in the top right corner of the console. Then click **[Abuses](https://console-sudo.infra.online.net/en/account/abuses/list)** in the pop-up menu.

<Lightbox src="scaleway-dedibox-console-abuses-menu.png" width="300" alt="" />

3. A list of your abuse tickets displays. Click **Details** to see the details of the complaint:

<Lightbox src="scaleway-dedibox-console-abuses-list.png" width="800" alt="" />

4. Take notice of the details and complete the steps necessary to resolve the problem. You can find information how to resolve [outgoing DDoS](https://www.scaleway.com/en/docs/how-to-prevent-outgoing-ddos/) attacks from your server or stop [sending malicious emails](https://www.scaleway.com/en/docs/stopping-outgoing-spam/) in our documentation.

5. Once the issue is solved, describe the measures you have taken and provide an answer to the author of the ticket. Click **Resolve** to mark the ticket as closed.

> **Note:** More information about Scaleway's Abuse Policy can be found [here](https://www.scaleway.com/en/abuse-notice/).
