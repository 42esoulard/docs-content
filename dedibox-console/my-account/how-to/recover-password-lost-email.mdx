For security reasons it is recommended that you verify your contact details reguarly and update your email address in case it changes to avoid being locked out from your account in case you have lost both, your Console password and aceess to your email account. 
In case this unlikely situation arrives, you can follow the following procedure to recover access to your Scaleway Dedibox account. 

<Message type="requirement">

  - You have an Scaleway Dedibox account
</Message>

This should never happen.
If nevertheless, this happens, and if you no longer have the access codes to your account, you must send us a request for the update of your email address contact with the following pieces of identification:

* Email address
* Postal address
* Two different documents proofing your identity (passport or local ID card for individual accounts + incorporation certificated for companies)
* A phone number to contact you
* Copy of your last Scaleway Dedibox invoice or the amount charged if you donâ€™t have a copy
* Card statement showing the Online by Scaleway bill debit

In any case, the request has to be sent by e-mail to:

<a href="mailto:document@scaleway.com">document@scaleway.com</a> 

We will update the e-mail address on receipt of the application, subject to validation.

Once your e-mail address has been updated, you can use [this form](https://console.online.net/en/user/recovery) to receive the recovery codes for your account.
